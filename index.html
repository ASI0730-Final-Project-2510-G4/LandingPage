About Us


Kinetic Systems Integration is the company behind FreelanceHub, a web platform designed to connect independent professionals with individuals and businesses in need of their services in a simple, direct, and secure way.

Our mission is to empower freelancers and specialized technicians by providing them with tools to showcase their work, build a strong professional reputation, and access real opportunities in their field.

Our vision is to become the most trusted and dynamic professional network in the freelance world, serving as the bridge between talent and need across any industry.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is FreelanceHub?

FreelanceHub is a web platform created by NovaLink Systems, designed to connect independent professionals with individuals and companies in need of their services in a simple, direct, and secure way. Our goal is to facilitate access to specialized talent and promote greater visibility for freelancers.

How can I register?

You can register by clicking the "Sign Up" button at the top of the page and filling out the form with your personal and professional information. Once registered, you'll be able to create your profile, showcase your portfolio, and start receiving job requests.

What benefits does FreelanceHub offer?

FreelanceHub offers access to specialized talent in various areas such as design, marketing, translation, and more. It also supports professional self-promotion, helping freelancers showcase their experience, portfolio, and ratings to attract new clients without intermediaries.

Is it easy to use?

Yes, FreelanceHub is easy to use, with an intuitive design that allows freelancers to create their profiles, access job opportunities, and coordinate directly with clients to agree on pricing, schedules, and work conditions.

How can I contact a freelancer directly?

FreelanceHub offers a direct communication channel between clients and professionals, allowing quick and personalized coordination to agree on pricing, location, and scheduling, ensuring clear and efficient agreements.

Does FreelanceHub charge any commission per transaction?

No, FreelanceHub does not charge any commission for transactions between clients and freelancers. However, we offer premium plans for freelancers who wish to access additional features, such as highlighting their profile or increasing their visibility on the platform.

How can I make sure a freelancer is reliable?

FreelanceHub allows freelancers to display their ratings and reviews from previous clients. You can check these reviews and ratings to ensure the freelancer has experience and a successful work history. We also offer a dispute resolution system to ensure all transactions are secure.

Can I hire freelancers for long-term work?

Yes, FreelanceHub allows clients to hire freelancers for both one-time projects and long-term work. You can establish an ongoing relationship with a freelancer by agreeing on terms and conditions that suit your needs.

Are there opportunities for freelancers in different industries?

Yes, FreelanceHub offers opportunities in a wide range of industries, including graphic design, web development, digital marketing, writing, translation, and more. Freelancers can explore job listings that match their skills and experience.
